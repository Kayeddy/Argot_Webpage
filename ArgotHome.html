Guess what!

Argot is an application that offers educational methods and tools that will allow users to learn English, emphasizing both on the grammar of the language as well as the listening and speech-fluidity so that talking to others can be way easier. There's a space for learners and also for those who would like to share their knowledge with the world. FIND YOUR ARGOT!



Here are few advantages of using our platform

Ensure your learning

Our platform makes sure you can make progress while learning, and students are given achievements medals for finishing activities.

Quality content

The library of information that we have is unique and provides content that is not easily found anywhere else. Furthermore, our teachers make sure that posted exercises are unique and directly emphasized on the subject the student is aiming to learn.

Flexibility

Students don't have to be subject to a static schedule, they can access the content from anywhere and at anytime they please.

Secure your progress and personal information

We store your data in our databases and we make sure only you can have access to it.
